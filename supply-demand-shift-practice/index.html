Review Demand and Supply Shifters

If you know the main shifters, the equilibrium predictions become much easier.

Demand Shifters

  • Price of substitutes
  • Price of complements
  • Income for a normal good
  • Income for an inferior good
  • Tastes and preferences
  • Expectations of future prices
  • Expectations of future income
  • Market size

Supply Shifters

  • Input prices
  • Technology
  • Expectations of future prices
  • Market size / number of firms
